This patent addresses high-frequency information loss in traditional Fourier Neural Operators for fluid dynamics PDE solving. The method proposes a frequency-domain mixture-of-experts architecture with gated sparse expert activation and progressive collaborative learning.

My contribution centered on the low-rank weight inheritance strategy that transfers pretrained low-frequency knowledge into lightweight high-frequency expert modules.
